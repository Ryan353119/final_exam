How to Recycle

Recycling helps reduce waste, conserve natural resources, and protect the environment.

Plastic Recycling

Clean plastic bottles and containers before recycling. Avoid mixing with food waste. Bring them to recycling centers.

Paper Recycling

Reuse paper when possible. Newspapers, magazines, and cardboard can be recycled into new paper products.

Composting

Turn food scraps like fruit peels and vegetable waste into compost for plants and gardens.

Metal Recycling

Aluminum cans and metal containers can be recycled many times without losing quality.

Glass Recycling

Glass bottles and jars can be reused or recycled. Separate by color if required in your area.

Reduce & Reuse

Before recycling, try to reduce waste and reuse items like bags, containers, and bottles.

Recycling is not just a simple task, it is a responsibility that we all share. In my opinion, many people ignore recycling because they think it is difficult or not important, but small actions can create big changes. If every person starts to separate their waste and reuse materials, we can greatly reduce pollution and protect nature.
