Avoid Throwing Trash Anywhere

Throwing trash anywhere can harm the environment, pollute water, and affect animals. Keeping surroundings clean helps create a safe and healthy place for everyone.

    Always throw trash in proper bins

    Keep rivers and streets clean

    Protect animals from harmful waste

    Encourage others to stay clean

Reminder: One small piece of trash can cause big environmental damage if not disposed of properly.

Discipline and responsibility can make a big difference. Clean habits lead to a cleaner future.
