Reduce Waste and Recycle Properly

Recycling helps reduce pollution, saves energy, and protects natural resources. By managing waste properly, we can create a cleaner and healthier environment.

Separate biodegradable and non-biodegradable waste
Reuse plastic containers and bottles
Recycle paper instead of throwing it away
Use eco-bags instead of plastic bags
Did you know?
Recycling one ton of paper can save 17 trees and thousands of liters of water.

Small habits like reusing and reducing waste can make a big difference. Start today and inspire others to do the same!
