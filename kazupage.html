Plant Trees and Take Care of Plants

Planting trees helps clean the air, provide oxygen, and give homes to animals. Taking care of plants also makes our surroundings beautiful and healthy.

You can start by planting small plants at home, watering them regularly, and protecting them from damage.

Why Plant Trees?

Trees help fight climate change by absorbing carbon dioxide. They also provide shade, reduce heat, and improve air quality for everyone.

Simple Ways to Help

    Water your plants regularly

    Use natural fertilizers

    Do not step on small plants

    Grow plants in recycled containers

Fun Fact: One tree can produce enough oxygen for 2–4 people every day!

Your Impact

Even small actions can make a big difference. If every person plants just one tree, we can help restore nature and protect wildlife habitats.
